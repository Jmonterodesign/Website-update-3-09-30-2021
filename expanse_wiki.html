DNA Enhanced Humans

Byzantium Enhanced

A genetically enhanced superhuman race that had their DNA altered and modified to become the “perfect super soldiers.” Each enhanced human receives a diluted dose of a serum that contains the DNA of the original “enhanced”. They develop similar features in having super strength but can develop their own unique set of physical powers. The majority of the enhanced have vulnerabilities to Byzantium. Enhanced are classified in a ranking system that groups them together by their overall raw power and training.

Byzantium Enhanced  (Mutated)

A rare subspecies of enhanced that is created when an error appears in the genome of the human receiving the dose. This creates a cannibalistic monster that has a continuous craving for human blood with no memory of who they were prior and no cure to the mutation.

Gene Enhanced

Humans that gained supernatural powers/ abilities after The World Power Conflict. The battle resulted in an explosion of a  genetic weapon that laced the atmosphere with radiation causing the genetic enhancement within the people of earth. Enhancements usually remain dormant within the body until activated by some sort of trauma.

Gene Augmented 

Technology has progressed to a point where humans are able to merge their physiology with animal DNA, augmenting them in some ways to exhibit traits of the respective animals. The process is in its infancy and tests on long term effects have only just begun. There is little information on mental health aspects of the transformation or further mutations which may occur, also there is no means to reverse the process as of yet. Most cases of transhuman DNA augmentation leave the subject infertile.

TOTEMS

Totems serve as prisons for spirits over the ages. If a host chooses to accept the totem, they will be granted a fraction of the beings power that is imprisoned along with its spirit. The host of the totem may grant full or partial control of its body to the being for a short period of time to be able to transform or use more of the being's power. A host given 100% of its body to the totem allows the spirit to transform into its original form.

Emerald Coyote

The prison in which the being Quelzar, a member of the Cur species. Whoever wields the totem is granted the ability of teleportation.

Saphire Orca

The prison in which the being Orcinus, a whale like being. Whoever wields the totem is granted the ability of hydrokinesis.

Topaz Owl

The prison in which the being Mestonia, from a race of avians. Whoever wields the totem is granted the ability to project illusions.

Universe Medals

The Universe Medals are objects found in the center of each planet and star of the cosmos. The bearer of one of these medals can control energy and elements of its origin planet or star by establishing a connection between them.

Sun Medal 

Is the medal from our solar system’s sun. With proper training, the bearer of this medal is managed with the ability to create fire and control it besides the super strength and resistance.

Earth Medal

Is the medal from our planet earth. It came from the center of the planet and with proper training, the bearer of this medal is managed with the ability to create water and control it besides the super strength and resistance.

Neptune Medal

Is the medal from the planet Neptune. It came from the center of the planet and with proper training, the bearer of this medal is managed with the ability to create strong winds and control them besides the super strength and resistance.

Saturn Medal

Is the medal from the planet saturn. It came from the center of the planet and with proper training, the bearer of this medal is managed with the ability to create rings  and control them besides the super strength and resistance.

Uranian Medal

Is the medal from the seventh planet of our Solar System. It came from the center of the planet and with proper training, the bearer of this medal is managed with the ability to create ice and control it besides the super strength and resistance.

Venus Medal

Is the medal from the planet Venus. It came from the center of the planet and with proper training, the bearer of this medal is managed with the ability to create heat and control it besides the super strength and resistance.

 Cygnus X-1 Medal

Is the medal from the Cygnus C-1 black hole. It came from the center of the black hole and with proper training, the bearer of this medal is managed with the ability to manipulate gravity besides the super strength and resistance.
